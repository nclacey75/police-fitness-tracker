Safari / iPad install steps

  1. Have IT upload this app folder to the department website.
  2. Open the app URL in Safari while online.
  3. Tap Share, then Add to Home Screen.
  4. Launch from the Home Screen icon later, even without internet.
  5. Before field use, test airplane-mode launch and PDF export on at least one iPad.
